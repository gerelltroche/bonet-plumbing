## Photo Guidelines for the Shoot

- **Lighting:** Natural light preferred. Avoid harsh shadows.
- **Branding:** Bonet Plumbing shirt/hat visible in all on-the-job shots.
- **Resolution:** Minimum 2000px on the long edge. Deliver as high-quality JPEG.
- **Orientation:** Landscape for hero/banner shots. Portrait for headshots/cards. Get both when possible.
- **File naming:** Descriptive names (e.g., `leo-water-heater-install.jpg`, `van-oviedo-driveway.jpg`).
- **Releases:** Get signed photo releases from any customers or recognizable properties.
- **Diversity of settings:** Mix of kitchens, bathrooms, utility rooms, exteriors.
- **Keep it real:** Authentic > overly staged. The current headshot strikes the right tone — professional but approachable.
