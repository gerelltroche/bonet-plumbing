## Tips for Getting Good Results from Gemini

1. **Upload Leo's reference photos** along with each prompt so Gemini can maintain his likeness.
2. **Include the van reference** in every scene that should show the van — AI doesn't remember between sessions.
3. **Specify "Central Florida residential setting"** to get the right architecture, vegetation, and lighting.
4. **Request landscape orientation (16:9)** for hero/banner images and **portrait/square** for card-style images.
5. **Ask for natural lighting** — Gemini tends to over-dramatize lighting if you don't specify.
6. **Iterate on branding** — Logo text on uniforms/vans may need a few tries. Provide the logo reference each time and ask Gemini to match it exactly.
7. **Generate multiple variations** of each shot and pick the best — AI images vary in quality per generation.
8. **Watch for AI artifacts** — Check hands, text on the van, tool details, and anything with fine text. These are where AI struggles most.
9. **Post-process if needed** — You can overlay the real logo onto the van/shirt in Canva or Photoshop if Gemini can't nail the text.
